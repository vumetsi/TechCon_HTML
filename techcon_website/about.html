History

TechCon was founded in 2010 with a vision to bring together innovators, technologists, and industry leaders from around the globe. Initially held in a modest community center in San Francisco, the conference has grown to become one of the most influential tech events, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually. TechCon has evolved alongside the tech industry, serving as a launchpad for groundbreaking ideas, technologies, and collaborations. Its journey reflects the rapid pace of innovation and the ever-expanding scope of technology in our lives.

Mission

  1. Inspire Innovation: Provide a platform for sharing cutting-edge ideas and breakthroughs in technology.
  2. Foster Collaboration: Create a space where developers, entrepreneurs, and corporations can connect and work together to solve complex challenges
  3. Empower Communities: Highlight the transformative power of technology to improve lives and address global issues.
  4. Celebrate Diversity: Amplify voices from underrepresented groups in tech to ensure a more inclusive future.
